# **写作模板：高分句型与词汇升级 (High-Scoring Writing Guide)**

针对 CET-4/6 和考研英语（Part A 应用文 / Part B 议论文），以下内容包含从词汇升级到篇章布局的全套方案。

## **1\. 词汇升级库 (Vocabulary Upgrade)**

别再用 "good", "bad", "think" 了，阅卷老师看腻了。

| 基础词 (Basic) | 高级替换词 (Advanced) | 语境/搭配 |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Good** | beneficial, favorable, rewarding, promising | The situation is *promising*. |
| **Bad** | detrimental, harmful, undesirable, adverse | *Adverse* effects. |
| **Important** | crucial, vital, significant, indispensable | Play a *vital* role. |
| **Think** | argue, assert, maintain, hold the view that | I *maintain* that... |
| **Many** | a host of, a multitude of, numerous | *A host of* problems. |
| **Show** | demonstrate, illustrate, reveal, indicate | The chart *illustrates*... |
| **Very** | exceedingly, extremely, remarkably | *Exceedingly* difficult. |
| **Cause (v.)** | give rise to, result in, lead to, trigger | *Trigger* a debate. |

## **2\. Part A: 应用文/书信模板 (Letter/Notice Writing)**

适用于考研英语小作文及四六级部分题目。

### **A. 建议信 (Letter of Suggestion)**

* **开头:** I am writing to express my views concerning \[Topic\] and offer some conducive suggestions. (我写信是为了表达关于...的看法并提供建议)  
* **建议部分:**  
  1. I would like to suggest that... (我想建议...)  
  2. It would be much better if you could... (如果你能...那就更好了)  
  3. My suggestion is that specific attention should be paid to... (我建议应特别关注...)  
* **结尾:** I hope you will find these proposals useful. (希望这些建议有用)

### **B. 道歉信 (Letter of Apology)**

* **开头:** I am writing to distinguishably apologize for my inability to \[do something\]. (我写信是为了对我未能...表示诚挚的歉意)  
* **解释原因:** The reason is that... (原因是...)  
* **补救措施:** To make up for the inconvenience caused, I would like to... (为了弥补造成的不便，我想...)  
* **结尾:** Once again, I am sorry for any inconvenience caused.

### **C. 通知 (Notice)**

* **标题:** NOTICE (居中)  
* **开头:** To \[Purpose\], a \[Activity Name\] will be held in \[Place\] on \[Date\]. (为了...，一场...活动将于...在...举行)  
* **细节:** Participants are required to... (参与者被要求...)  
* **结尾:** Everyone is welcome to participate. (欢迎大家参加)  
* **落款:** \[Organization Name\] (右下角)

## **3\. Part B: 图画/图表描述段 (Describing Charts/Images)**

* **开篇引出:**  
  * As is vividly shown in the chart/cartoon, ... (如图表/漫画生动所示...)  
  * What strikes us most in the picture is that... (图片中最引人注目的是...)  
* **描述数据变化:**  
  * The number of \[Subject\] **experienced a dramatic increase** from \[Year\] to \[Year\].  
  * There has been a **slight decline** in the percentage of...  
  * \[Subject\] **accounts for** the largest proportion, standing at 65%.

## **4\. Part B: 观点阐述段 (Argumentation)**

* **引出原因 (Causal Analysis):**  
  * Several factors contribute to this phenomenon. First and foremost, ...  
  * The primary reason behind this is that...  
  * Usually, \[Subject\] stems from... (...源于...)  
* **连接词 (Transitions):**  
  * On one hand, ... On the other hand, ...  
  * In addition / Furthermore / Moreover  
  * Conversely / However (转折)

## **5\. Part B: 结论建议段 (Conclusion)**

* **归纳总结:**  
  * In summary / To conclude / In a nutshell, ...  
  * From what has been discussed above, we may reasonably arrive at the conclusion that...  
* **提出建议:**  
  * It is high time that we **took effective measures to**...  
  * Only by doing \[X\] can we solve this problem fundamentally.

## **6\. 万能功能句 (Functional Sentences)**

* **强调句:** It is \[knowledge\] **that** plays a crucial role in our life.  
* **双重否定:** It is **not uncommon** to see that... (看到...是很常见的)  
* **倒装句:** **Not only** does technology facilitate our life, **but** it **also** brings potential risks.
